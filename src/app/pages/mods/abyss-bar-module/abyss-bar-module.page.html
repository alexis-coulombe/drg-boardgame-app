Abyss bar module

After choosing a mission but before deploying, the group decides whether or not to order a round. If they do, the drink effect applies to all dwarves for the entire mission. Beer effects do not apply to Bosco, he can't drink!


If a beer was chosen, move the hazard marker up one space before the mission begins. Additionally, do no draw a rock and stone card when picking up a barley bulb token. The Abyss bar needs those to restock all the beer you just drank!
